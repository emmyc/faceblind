Says Alice to Humpty Dumpty, “Till we meet again,”  

“I shouldn’t know you again if we did meet... you're so exactly like other people.”

Alice answers, “The face is what one goes by, generally.”

He adds, “That’s just what I complain of…your face is the same as everybody…

Now if you had the two eyes on the same side of the nose…

…or the mouth at the top—that would be some help.”
